═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TechCorp offers three medical plan options through Blue Cross Blue Shield (BCBS):

TechCorp contributes the following to employee HSA accounts (HDHP plan):
  • Employee-only enrollment: $600/year
  • Employee + Family enrollment: $1,200/year

In-Network Co-pays (Select PPO):
  • Primary Care Visit: $20
  • Specialist Visit: $40
  • Urgent Care: $50
  • Emergency Room: $250 (waived if admitted)
  • Rx Generic: $10 | Preferred Brand: $35 | Non-Preferred: $70

Open Enrollment: November 1–15 each year. Changes take effect January 1.
To change your plan outside open enrollment, you must have a qualifying life event
(marriage, birth, loss of other coverage). Submit within 30 days of the event.

2. DENTAL & VISION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Dental (Delta Dental):
  • Preventive care (cleanings, X-rays): 100% covered, no deductible
  • Basic restorative (fillings): 80% covered after $100 deductible
  • Major restorative (crowns, root canals): 50% covered after deductible
  • Orthodontia: 50% covered, $2,500 lifetime maximum
  • Annual maximum benefit: $2,000 per person
  • Employee premium: $12/month | Family: $38/month

Vision (VSP):
  • Annual eye exam: $15 copay
  • Frames: $150 allowance every 24 months
  • Contact lenses: $150 allowance every 12 months (in lieu of frames)
  • Employee premium: $5/month | Family: $14/month

3. LIFE INSURANCE & DISABILITY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Basic Life Insurance:
  • TechCorp provides 2x annual salary in life insurance (up to $500,000) at no cost.
  • Supplemental life insurance is available up to 5x salary; evidence of insurability
    required for amounts over 3x salary.

Short-Term Disability (STD):
  • 60% of weekly base salary, up to $2,500/week
  • Begins after a 7-calendar-day elimination period
  • Maximum benefit duration: 13 weeks
  • Fully employer-paid

Long-Term Disability (LTD):
  • 60% of monthly base salary, up to $10,000/month
  • Begins after STD benefits are exhausted (after 13 weeks)
  • Benefit continues until recovery, age 65, or death (whichever is first)
  • Fully employer-paid

4. 401(K) RETIREMENT PLAN
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Provider: Fidelity Investments
Enrollment: Automatic at 3% contribution on Day 1. You may change anytime.

TechCorp Match:
  • 100% match on your contributions up to 4% of your base salary
  • Match is deposited each payroll period (not annually)

Vesting Schedule:
  • Your own contributions: 100% immediately vested
  • TechCorp match: cliff vesting — 100% vested after 2 years of service

2025 IRS Contribution Limits:
  • Employee contributions: $23,000/year
  • Catch-up contributions (age 50+): additional $7,500/year

Investment options include target-date funds, Fidelity index funds, and a
self-directed brokerage window. Access your account at https://nb.fidelity.com/techcorp.
