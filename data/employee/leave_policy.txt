TECHCORP INC. — LEAVE & TIME OFF POLICY
Confidential — For Employees Only
Effective: January 1, 2025

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1. PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TechCorp uses a tenure-based PTO accrual model:

  Years of Service        Annual PTO Days     Accrual Rate (per pay period)
  ────────────────────    ───────────────     ─────────────────────────────
  0 – 2 years             15 days             0.577 days
  3 – 5 years             20 days             0.769 days
  6 – 10 years            25 days             0.962 days
  10+ years               Unlimited PTO       N/A (manager approval still required)

PTO begins accruing from Day 1. A maximum of 10 days may be carried over to the
next calendar year; any surplus is forfeited on January 31 each year.

Upon separation from the company, accrued but unused PTO is paid out in full in
your final paycheck (applicable in all states where required by law, and by default
for all US employees at TechCorp).

How to Request PTO:
  1. Submit your request in Workday ≥ 2 weeks in advance for absences of 3+ days.
  2. For single-day absences, notify your manager by 8 AM the morning of the absence.
  3. PTO during major project milestones or release dates requires manager sign-off.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
2. SICK LEAVE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Sick leave is separate from PTO and does not accrue or expire.
Employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year (not carried over).

Sick leave may be used for:
  • Your own illness, injury, or medical appointments
  • Care for an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent)
  • Mental health days

Doctor's note may be required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3. PARENTAL LEAVE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Primary Caregiver Leave:
  • 16 weeks fully paid (100% of base salary) for birth, adoption, or foster placement
  • Must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event
  • Eligible from Day 1 of employment (no waiting period)

Secondary Caregiver Leave:
  • 4 weeks fully paid
  • Must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event

Notification: Notify your manager and People Ops at least 30 days before the expected
start date when possible. Contact parentalleave@techcorp.com for paperwork.

Parental leave does not count against PTO balances.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 days paid
  Extended family (grandparent, grandchild, in-law): 3 days paid
  Other relationships: 1 day paid; additional days may be taken as PTO

Bereavement leave may be extended at manager discretion for travel requirements.
Submit documentation (if requested) to people@techcorp.com.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6. JURY DUTY & CIVIC LEAVE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TechCorp provides up to 10 paid days for jury duty. Employees serving on juries
lasting longer than 10 days may use accrued PTO for additional days. Show your
summons to HR and keep your manager informed of expected duration.

Voting leave: Up to 2 hours of paid time off to vote in federal, state, and local
elections where polls are not accessible before or after working hours.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
7. UNPAID LEAVE OF ABSENCE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Employees who have exhausted all paid leave may request an unpaid leave of absence
for personal reasons (subject to manager and HR approval). Maximum duration: 90 days.
Benefits continue during approved unpaid leave (employee cost-share still applies).

FMLA: Eligible employees (12 months tenure, 1,250+ hours worked) may take up to
12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave under the Family and Medical Leave Act.
Contact people@techcorp.com to initiate FMLA paperwork.
