TECHCORP INC. — EMPLOYEE FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)
Last Updated: March 2025

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
GETTING STARTED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: What is TechCorp's main product?
A: TechCorp builds DataPilot — an enterprise data analytics platform. We also offer
   AutoFlow, our workflow automation suite. Combined, they serve over 400 enterprise
   clients across North America and Europe.

Q: Who do I contact on my first day?
A: Your hiring manager will send a Day-1 checklist. Your onboarding buddy (assigned
   by People Ops) will also reach out the week before you start. For IT setup, email
   helpdesk@techcorp.com with your employee ID.

Q: Where is the employee portal?
A: https://portal.techcorp.internal — accessible via VPN or from the office network.
   It links to payroll (ADP), benefits (Benefitfocus), IT tickets, and expense reports.

Q: How do I access the company Slack?
A: You'll receive an invitation to your TechCorp email address. Download the Slack
   desktop app and sign in with Google SSO using your @techcorp.com account.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PAYROLL & COMPENSATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: When do I get paid?
A: TechCorp pays bi-weekly (every other Friday). Your first paycheck may be delayed
   by one pay cycle depending on your start date.

Q: How do I set up direct deposit?
A: Log in to ADP (adp.techcorp.internal) → Payment → Banking Information → Add account.
   Allow 1–2 pay cycles for the change to take effect.

Q: Where can I find my pay stubs?
A: All pay stubs and W-2s are available in ADP under "Pay" → "Pay Statements."

Q: Who handles payroll questions?
A: payroll@techcorp.com or call +1 (512) 555-0150 Mon–Fri 9 AM–5 PM CT.

BENEFITS OVERVIEW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: When do my benefits start?
A: Benefits are effective on your first day of employment. You have 30 days from your
   start date to make benefits elections in Benefitfocus; otherwise you'll be enrolled
   in default plans until the next Open Enrollment window (November each year).

Q: What health insurance plans does TechCorp offer?
A: TechCorp offers three medical plan tiers through BCBS:
   - TechCorp Basic PPO
   - TechCorp Select PPO (most popular)
   - TechCorp HDHP + HSA
   For plan details and premiums, see the Benefits Guide (employee-only document).

Q: Does TechCorp offer a 401(k)?
A: Yes. TechCorp matches 100% of your contributions up to 4% of your salary.
   The 401(k) is administered by Fidelity. You may enroll or adjust contributions
   at any time via https://nb.fidelity.com/techcorp.

Q: Is there a gym/wellness benefit?
A: Yes — TechCorp reimburses up to $600/year in eligible gym or fitness expenses.
   Submit receipts through Concur with the category "Wellness Reimbursement."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
LEAVE & TIME OFF
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: How many PTO days do I get?
A: PTO accrual is based on tenure. See the Leave Policy document for full details.
   All PTO balances are visible in the employee portal under "Time & Attendance."

Q: How do I request time off?
A: Submit PTO requests in the portal (Workday) → "Time Off" → "Request." Requests
   of 3+ consecutive days require at least 2 weeks advance notice (non-emergency).

Q: Are there paid company holidays?
A: Yes. TechCorp observes 11 US federal holidays plus 3 floating holidays each year.
   The full holiday calendar is published in the employee portal in November for the
   following year.

LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Does TechCorp support professional development?
A: Every employee receives a $1,500 annual Learning & Development (L&D) budget for
   courses, certifications, books, and conferences. Submit reimbursement requests
   through Concur using the "L&D" category with manager pre-approval.

Q: Are there internal training programs?
A: Yes — "TechCorp University" (TCU) on our internal LMS offers courses on our products,
   technical skills, leadership, and compliance. Access via https://tcu.techcorp.internal.

REPORTING CONCERNS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: How do I report a workplace concern anonymously?
A: Use our Ethics Hotline: ethics@techcorp.com or 1-800-555-ETHICS (available 24/7).
   EthicsPoint, our third-party reporting platform, is also available at
   https://www.ethicspoint.com/techcorp (fully anonymous option available).

Q: What is TechCorp's anti-harassment policy?
A: TechCorp has a zero-tolerance policy for any form of harassment or discrimination.
   All complaints are investigated promptly and confidentially. Retaliation against
   anyone who reports in good faith is strictly prohibited. See the full policy in
   Confluence under Legal/Policies/Anti-Harassment.
