1. WELCOME TO TECHCORP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Welcome to TechCorp Inc.! We are a software technology company founded in 2015,
headquartered in Austin, Texas, with offices in New York, London, and Bangalore.
We build enterprise data analytics and automation platforms serving Fortune 500 clients.

Our Mission: Empower organizations with intelligent software that transforms raw data
into clear, actionable decisions.

Our Core Values:
  • Integrity First — We do the right thing, even when it's hard.
  • Customer Obsession — We succeed when our customers succeed.
  • Innovation Without Fear — We encourage bold ideas and calculated risks.
  • Inclusive Excellence — Diverse teams build better products.
  • Continuous Learning — We invest in growing every TechCorp team member.

2. CODE OF CONDUCT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

All TechCorp employees are expected to:

  a) Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism.
  b) Protect confidential company and customer information.
  c) Disclose and avoid conflicts of interest.
  d) Comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
  e) Report suspected violations through our anonymous Ethics Hotline:
     ethics@techcorp.com | 1-800-555-ETHICS

Harassment, discrimination, retaliation, or any form of workplace violence will
not be tolerated and may result in immediate termination.

3. WORKING HOURS & LOCATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Standard Hours: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday–Friday (local time).
TechCorp operates a hybrid work model — employees are expected to be in office
a minimum of 2 days per week (Tuesday and Thursday preferred).

Remote Work: Full-remote arrangements require manager approval and are documented
in your employment agreement addendum.

Core Collaboration Hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM your local timezone — all employees
should be reachable during this window.

Overtime: Non-exempt employees must obtain prior written manager approval for
any hours beyond 40 per week. Exempt employees are not eligible for overtime pay.

5. IT & EQUIPMENT POLICY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

All employees receive a company-provisioned laptop. Personal devices may be used
for email via the TechCorp mobile app (MDM enrollment required).

Software Installation: Only IT-approved software may be installed on company devices.
Submit requests via the IT Portal at https://it.techcorp.internal.

Data Storage: Company data must reside in approved systems (Google Workspace,
Confluence, or designated cloud storage). Personal cloud accounts (Dropbox, personal
Google Drive) are not authorized for company data.

Security: Employees must use VPN when accessing internal resources from outside
the office. MFA is mandatory on all company accounts.

IT Support: helpdesk@techcorp.com | Ext. 1234 | Mon–Fri 8 AM–8 PM ET

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Pre-approved business expenses are reimbursed within 15 business days of submission.
Submit via Concur (expenses.techcorp.internal) with receipts attached.

Meal limits: $25/person (team lunch), $75/person (client entertainment).
Travel must be booked via our corporate travel portal (Navan) to qualify for
reimbursement. Personal bookings are reimbursed at coach/standard rates only.
